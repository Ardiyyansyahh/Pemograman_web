The Best Hotels

ASTON Batam Hotel & Residence is conveniently located in the heart of Batam City, providing easy access to both business and leisure destinations. The hotel is just a few minutes away from Nagoya Hill Shopping Mall, one of the most popular retail and entertainment centers in the city. It is also close to the Harbor Bay Ferry Terminal, offering quick connections to Singapore and nearby islands. The hotel’s central location makes it an ideal choice for travelers who want to explore the vibrant city life, enjoy various dining and shopping options, or conduct business in Batam’s commercial district. Whether visiting for work or relaxation, guests will appreciate the convenience and comfort that ASTON Batam offers.
